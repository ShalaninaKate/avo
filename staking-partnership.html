APR — Annual Percentage Rate is a standardized metric used to express the cost of the potential return on staking a certain amount of cryptocurrency over a year.

TVL — Total value locked is a metric used in the cryptocurrency sector to determine the total U.S. dollar value of digital assets staked via decentralized finance (DeFi) platforms or decentralized applications (dApps). The higher the TVL of a project, the more secure and valuable it is perceived to be.
